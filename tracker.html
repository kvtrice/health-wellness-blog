Mood-Sleep Tracker

Studies show that quality sleep plays a critical role in maintaining mental and emotional well-being. When we sleep well, our bodies undergo restorative processes that regulate mood, improve cognitive function, and reduce stress. On the flip side, poor sleep can lead to increased irritability, anxiety, and even depression.

By using our Mood and Sleep Tracker, you can gain some useful insights into how your sleep quality impacts your mood over time. Tracking this data helps you identify patterns and make informed adjustments to your sleep habits, ultimately enhancing your overall mental health and well-being!
